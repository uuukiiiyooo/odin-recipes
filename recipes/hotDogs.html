Hot dogs

Hot cakes are an easy and delicious breakfast.

They mix with everything from bacon to berries and honey!

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Fluor

Let's make some!

  1. Combine the fluor, milk and eggs
  2. Melt the butter
  3. Beat the mix unti uniform
  4. Melt some more butter on a pan
  5. Slowly add the mix to the hot pan
  6. Serve
